Based on Islamic Principles

"It is not permissible for any Muslim who has something to bequeath to stay for two nights without having his will written." — Sahih al-Bukhari & Muslim

This system follows the order of estate distribution as prescribed in Islamic law:

  1. Funeral Expenses — First priority
  2. Debt Settlement — All debts must be paid
  3. Wasiyyah (Bequests) — Up to 1/3 to non-heirs
  4. Inheritance Distribution — According to Al-Faraid
